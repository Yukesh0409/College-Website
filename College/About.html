Core Values

St.Joseph’s is committed in practices that are fair, honest and objective in dealing with students, faculty members and other stakeholders, which fosters a climate of ethical conduct, respect, responsibility and Trust. We believe in stakeholder partnership for holistic institutional development and to promote a healthier working atmosphere with the following core values:

Reverence

  1. To fulfil our mission of reaching newer heights through multiple practices by fostering an inclusive and caring environment.
  2. To respect and value the individual diversity, allowing to harness the richness of ideas and perspectives for the benefit of the whole staff and student community.
  3. To keep up confidence on the stakeholders and recognize their support to show gratitude to the stakeholder’s contribution

Excellence

  1. To promote academic excellence and encourage innovation among students and staffs
  2. To exhibit quality in recruitment, infrastructure facilities and service
  3. To sustain interactive teaching learning process

Liability

  1. To keep transparency in administrative functions
  2. To take responsibility for professional growth and development
  3. To continuously evaluate the systems and policies

Discipline

  1. To inculcate human values among students
  2. To promote gender equity, Indian culture and traditions
  3. To mould our students as responsible citizens of the nation
